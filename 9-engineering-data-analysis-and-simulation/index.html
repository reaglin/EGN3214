EGN3214 — Course Module

9 – Engineering Data Analysis and Simulation

This module guides you through the end-to-end engineering data pipeline—from loading and cleaning raw datasets with pandas and NumPy, to visualizing results with matplotlib, to building numerical simulations of mechanical, electrical, and thermal systems using ODE-based time-stepping methods. Use the resources below to navigate readings and supporting materials.
