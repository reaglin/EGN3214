Module 6 — Data Visualization with Matplotlib and Intro to Pandas

This module introduces Python-based data visualization using Matplotlib and explores the foundational data-handling capabilities of the Pandas library. Learners will progress from creating basic plots to building publication-quality figures, and from understanding Pandas data structures to filtering, sorting, and summarizing real engineering datasets.
